About Me

My name is Esther Adegoke, and I joined Altschool in September 2023. My journey to Altschool is quite interesting as I had applied to The School of Engineering earlier in the year but I couldn't complete due to no funds. I reapplied later in the year and I was able to secure a 50% scholarship to learn at Altschool. I have no prior background in programming nor computer science. My background was in politics, music and content writing. However, I love problem solving and Maths so much that I would randomly look up quizes online. I found out I could keep providing solutions to problems through programming and after researching how I could start, I found Altschool.

Why Alschool?
